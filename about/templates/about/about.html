About Us

The Garden Path

At The Garden Path, we're more than just garden care professionals - we're passionate gardeners who love helping people create and maintain beautiful, thriving outdoor spaces. With years of experience in garden care and maintenance, we've become a trusted name across the western Munster region for delivering exceptional service and stunning results.

Our Story

The Garden Path was founded 15 years ago by Dermot Murphy, a lifelong gardening enthusiast with a passion for transforming outdoor spaces. Dermot grew up surrounded by nature, cultivating his love for plants and gardening from a young age. After years of honing his skills and working in garden care, he decided to start The Garden Path to share his expertise with the local community. Known for his attention to detail and deep knowledge of plants, Dermot built the company on the principles of personalized service, reliability, and a genuine love for nurturing gardens. Today, his legacy of exceptional garden care continues to flourish with a dedicated team that shares his commitment to quality.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help our clients enjoy their outdoor spaces without the stress of upkeep. Whether it's regular grass care, precise hedge trimming, or ensuring your flowerbeds bloom year-round, we take pride in delivering top-quality service that exceeds expectations. We believe that every garden deserves to look its best, and we're here to make that happen.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experience: With years of hands-on experience in garden care, we've developed the skills and knowledge to handle gardens of all shapes and sizes.
  • Personalized Service: We understand that every garden is unique, and we tailor our services to meet your garden's specific needs.
  • Reliability: Our clients trust us for our dependable, timely service and our commitment to delivering consistent results.
  • Sustainability: We care about the environment as much as we do about your garden. That's why we use eco-friendly practices and organic products whenever possible to keep your garden healthy and green.
